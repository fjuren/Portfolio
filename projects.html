Dice Game

This is a simple web-based game that lets you and your friend, colleague or family member finally resolve that one thing that you always argue about. The rules here are simple. First, agree who player 1 and player 2 is. Then click on the green re-roll button and voila, you finally have your disagreement resolved forever! If you tie, well maybe call it best out of 3? Have fun!

Languages used:

- HTML

- CSS

- Javascript

Drum Kit

This is another web-based game that lets you practice your drumming skills. To play, choose to either click on the icons using your mouse or use the keys on your keyboard to play the corresponding drum sound. Don't forget to turn your volume up to max!
